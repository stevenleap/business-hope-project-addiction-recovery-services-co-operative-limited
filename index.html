About Hope Project

Hope Project Addiction Recovery Services Co-operative Limited promotes health by providing individuals suffering from psychological trauma and substance use disorder with access to individual counseling, rehabilitation information and classes, and group support programs. Located in Point Edward, Nova Scotia, we are a community-focused nonprofit dedicated to fostering recovery.

Serving Point Edward and surrounding communities in Nova Scotia.
